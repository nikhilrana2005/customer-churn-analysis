==================================================
BUSINESS INSIGHTS
=================

Key Churn Drivers:

1. Contract Risk
   Customers with shorter contracts are more likely to churn.

2. Fiber Optic Internet Service
   Customers using Fiber Optic services show higher churn tendencies.

3. Customer Tenure
   Newer customers are more likely to leave compared to long-term customers.

4. Payment Method
   Customers using Electronic Check demonstrate higher churn rates.

5. Security & Support Services
   Customers without Online Security and Technical Support are more likely to churn.

==================================================
RECOMMENDATIONS
===============

1. Promote long-term contracts through discounts and loyalty rewards.

2. Target high-risk customers with personalized retention campaigns.

3. Encourage customers to adopt Online Security and Technical Support services.

4. Monitor Fiber Optic customers closely and improve service quality.

5. Offer incentives for automatic payment methods to reduce churn.
