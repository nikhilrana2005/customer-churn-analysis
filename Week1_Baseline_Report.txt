# Customer Churn Prediction & LTV Engine

## Week 1 Baseline Analytics Report

### Project Objective

The objective of this analysis is to identify customer churn patterns using demographic, billing, contract, and service-related data. The insights generated during Week 1 will be used for predictive modeling in Week 2.

### Dataset Overview

* Dataset: Telco Customer Churn Dataset
* Total Records: 7,043 Customers
* Total Features: 21 Variables
* Target Variable: Churn (Yes/No)

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Customer Churn Overview

* A significant portion of customers have churned, indicating a need for proactive retention strategies.
* Most customers remain active, providing sufficient data for churn prediction modeling.

### 2. Contract Type Analysis

* Customers with Month-to-Month contracts exhibit the highest churn rate.
* Customers with One-Year and Two-Year contracts are significantly more loyal.
* Long-term contracts reduce churn risk and improve customer retention.

### 3. Tenure Analysis

* Customers with lower tenure are more likely to churn.
* Long-tenure customers demonstrate stronger loyalty and lower cancellation rates.
* Early customer engagement is critical during the first year of service.

### 4. Monthly Charges Analysis

* Customers paying higher monthly charges tend to churn more frequently.
* Pricing sensitivity appears to influence customer retention decisions.
* High-value customers should be targeted with personalized retention offers.

### 5. Payment Method Analysis

* Customers using Electronic Check show relatively higher churn rates.
* Customers using automatic payment methods tend to remain longer with the company.
* Encouraging auto-pay adoption may improve retention.

### 6. Internet Service Analysis

* Fiber Optic customers show higher churn compared to other internet service categories.
* Further investigation is required to determine whether pricing, service quality, or competition is driving churn.

### 7. Tech Support Analysis

* Customers without Tech Support churn significantly more than those who subscribe to support services.
* Technical assistance appears to increase customer satisfaction and retention.

### 8. Online Security Analysis

* Customers without Online Security services are more likely to churn.
* Security-related services contribute positively to customer retention.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Launch targeted retention campaigns for Month-to-Month customers.

### Recommendation 2

Provide onboarding programs and incentives for new customers during their first year.

### Recommendation 3

Offer discounts, loyalty rewards, or bundled plans to customers with high monthly charges.

### Recommendation 4

Promote automatic payment enrollment to reduce churn risk.

### Recommendation 5

Bundle Tech Support and Online Security services with internet plans to improve retention.

### Recommendation 6

Investigate Fiber Optic customer feedback to identify dissatisfaction drivers.

---

## Week 1 Outcome

The exploratory data analysis successfully identified key factors influencing customer churn, including:

* Contract Type
* Customer Tenure
* Monthly Charges
* Payment Method
* Internet Service Type
* Tech Support
* Online Security

These findings provide the foundation for Week 2, where machine learning models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost) will be developed to predict churn and estimate customer lifetime value (LTV).
